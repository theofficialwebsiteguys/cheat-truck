Our Story

Brother Bruno Pizza, aka THE #CHEATMEALHEADQUARTERS, the oldest family-owned and operated pizzeria in New Jersey, is expanding its reach with our mobile pizza kitchen: The #CheatTruck. Known for our authentic Italian recipes and commitment to quality, Brother Bruno Pizza brings a taste of New Jersey's finest right to you.

With decades of experience and a loyal customer base, we plan to open in every city throughout the U.S. We offer a variety of options, including traditional pizza, unique dessert pizzas, and a CHEAT MEAL experience like no other.

Meet Brother Bruno

Born and raised in New Jersey, Bruno grew up in the pizzeria business—learning the value of hard work, family, and unforgettable food.

Today, he continues that legacy with The #CheatTruck, bringing people together through bold flavors, good vibes, and a CHEAT MEAL experience you won't forget.
